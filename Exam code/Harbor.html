The Harbor

In the heart of Aarhus, you can find the marina and
the old wood ship harbor. The marina was
concentrated on May 14th of 1933. There was a big
party with thousands of participants. People used to
call it the norths most beautiful marina.

The wood ship harbor has the biggest selection of
wooden vessels in all of Denmark. You can find
around 90 classic ships and boats that are declared
worthy of preservation as Danish maritime cultural
heritage. All the boats are more than 100 years old,
but they all still work.
There are also some nice restaurants located by the
marina that mainly focuses on fish. When you walk
around the marina you can smell the fish being
prepared. On a windy day it is like the ships are
talking or screaming.
