Best Restaurants In Town

Here are five places where you must eat in Aarhus

Toast It

Toast it is a little cozy sandwich shop, it is
both great for breakfast and lunch. It is a
family-owned corporation. Their sandwiches
are made on homemade sourdough bread that
has been in the making foe at least 48 hours.
They only use fresh and tasty ingredients.

Vaca

Vaca serves Mexican finger food. They
serve everything from tacos to ensalada
that you can enjoy with a margherita or try
one of their Mexican beers.

Aarhus Street Food

Aarhus Street Food is the place to go when
you cannot agree on where to eat. Aarhus
Street Food has more than 30 kitchens and
bars. There is everything from Danish to
Caribbean food to homemade popsicles.
KöD
KöD serves the best steaks from all over
the world. You can pick from all the
delicious sides to go with your steak. They
also have good quality wine to go with the
atmosphere.
Domestic
Domestic specializes in finer Nordic
cooking and they care about local
ingredients, animal welfare and
sustainability and get supplies from
surrounding farms for the food. You can
choose between four or eight courses with
a wine or juice menu. The meal begins with
a string of appetizers, the dessert menu is
very unique.
